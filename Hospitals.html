Top Hospitals in Hyderabad

Healthcare facilities are one of the things that can make or break your decision to move to a new city. But don’t worry, the hospitals in Hyderabad have got you covered. Plus, here’s the thing, with more and more folks moving to the city every year, the demand for healthcare services has increased. And so has the number of top hospitals in Hyderabad to meet that demand.

1. Apollo Hospital

Apollo Hospital is a world-class hospital with state-of-the-art facilities and the best specialists in Hyderabad. The hospital is known for its excellence in several fields, including cardiology, neurology, urology, and oncology. The hospital has 550 beds, 160 critical care beds, and 18 operating theaters, making it the first of the top 10 hospitals in Hyderabad.

2. Yashoda Hospital

Yashoda Hospital is another top-ranking multi-specialty hospital in Hyderabad. The hospital has many awards for its clinical excellence and patient care. The hospital has 1200 beds, 300 critical care beds, and 24 dedicated surgical suites. They have an exceptional team of doctors and surgeons who are experts in several specialties, including cardiology, oncology, gastroenterology, neurology, and pulmonology.
